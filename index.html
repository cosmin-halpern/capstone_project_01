Short Summary

Harry Potter is a courageous young wizard best known for surviving an attack by the dark wizard Lord Voldemort as an infant. Raised by his unkind relatives, the Dursleys, Harry discovers his magical heritage on his eleventh birthday and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Throughout his years at Hogwarts, he becomes known for his bravery, loyalty, and strong sense of justice, forming close friendships with Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley. Ultimately, Harry plays a central role in the fight against Voldemort, symbolizing hope, friendship, and the triumph of good over evil.
